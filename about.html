Hey, I’m Alberto de Lucas.

A holistic product designer passionate about creating meaningful and useful digital experiences with a focus on visual design, who is based in Madrid.

Design Principles

01.

Simplicity

Designs should be simple, with a clear and intuitive interface that allows users to easily achieve their goals without frustration.

02.

Empathy

Consider the perspective of the users and design with their needs and goals in mind.

03.

Consistency

Provide an internally consistent experience by using familiar standards and paradigms people are comfortable with.

04.

Human

Place people at the heart of the design process.

I take a holistic and humanistic approach to everything I do, connecting all areas of design in order to create meaningful experiences and valuable products that improve people’s lives.

With more than 7 years of experience working in print, product, branding and marketing campaigns for known companies such as Iberdrola, Mastercard or the Spanish Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Working with a wide range of companies and their brands, in addition to fast-paced agile environments made me a much more flexible professional ready to solve complex design problems efficiently.

I am always motivated to discover and experiment with new disciplines and tools.
